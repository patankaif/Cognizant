Local News Bulletin

new community center grand opening

The new city council community center will open its doors next Saturday. The facility features a modern computer lab, a fully-equipped woodworking studio, and dedicated classrooms for community workshops. Everyone is invited to the ribbon-cutting ceremony and the afternoon barbecue.

annual street market registrations open

Registrations for stalls at the annual summer street market are now open to local businesses and artisans. The market is scheduled for August 15 and is expected to attract over five thousand visitors. Local garden growers and home chefs are highly encouraged to participate.
